This is Doge

This page will be dedicated to my sanity as of late. This fuzz ball of joy is my Husky North. She has provided me comfort and joy in the darkest of times. She is more than just a pet with unconditional love, but a source of companionship I had long forgotten. Growing up in the country I had a plethora of pets ranging from guinea pigs to horses, wild and tame, but in my opinion nothing can replace the friendship that can be shared between a human and a canine.
